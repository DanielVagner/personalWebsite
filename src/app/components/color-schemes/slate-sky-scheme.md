# Slate + Sky Color Scheme (Minimalistický & Čistý)

## Primary Colors
- **Slate**: slate-50, slate-100, slate-200, slate-600, slate-700, slate-800, slate-900, slate-950
- **Sky**: sky-50, sky-100, sky-200, sky-400, sky-500, sky-600, sky-700, sky-800, sky-900, sky-950

## Accent Colors
- **Amber**: amber-100, amber-400, amber-500, amber-600, amber-700, amber-900 (Backend)
- **Emerald**: emerald-100, emerald-400, emerald-600, emerald-900 (Tools)
- **Red**: red-100, red-400, red-600, red-900 (Email)

## Gradients
- Hero: `from-slate-50 to-sky-50` (light) / `from-slate-900 to-slate-800` (dark)
- Avatar: `from-slate-600 to-sky-600`
- Contact: `from-slate-50 to-sky-100` (light) / `from-slate-900 to-slate-800` (dark)
- Philosophy: `from-slate-50 to-sky-50` (light) / `from-slate-950 to-sky-950` (dark)
- Projects Images: `from-slate-100 to-sky-100` (light) / `from-slate-900 to-sky-900` (dark)
- Availability Card: `from-sky-500 to-sky-600`

## Borders & Highlights
- Featured cards: `border-sky-200` / `dark:border-sky-800`
- Current badge: `bg-green-600`
- Project borders: `border-sky-200` / `dark:border-sky-800`

## Pocit & Asociace
- **Pocit**: Profesionální, elegantní, čistý, minimalistický
- **Asociace**: Enterprise, kvalita, spolehlivost, moderní architektura
- **Vhodné pro**: Seriózní architect role, korporátní prostředí

## Usage Map
- **Hero section**: Slate-sky gradient background, slate-sky avatar gradient
- **Skills section**: Sky pro Frontend, Amber pro Backend, Emerald pro Tools
- **Experience section**: Sky accents, green "Current" badge
- **Projects section**: Slate-sky gradient na obrázky
- **About section**: Sky accents, slate-sky philosophy section
- **Contact section**: Slate-sky gradients, sky CTA card
